About



Quiz Run is a quiz competition comprising of 70 questions. The questions will be from the following sections: Science and Technology, Art and Literature, Sports, Movies and Entertainment, Mathematics, Geography and Current Affairs. The details of the questions are given below.

Questions      Difficulty Level      Marking Scheme     
1-35 Easy +2, -1
36-55 Medium +3, -1
56-70 Hard +5, -0


Rules & Regulations


  • Once you register, you will be redirected to the questions page.
  • You can attempt each question only once. You would not get any second chance in case of wrong attempt
  • You may skip any question keeping in mind that you would not get any second chance
  • When you login next time, you will be redirected to where you last left off
